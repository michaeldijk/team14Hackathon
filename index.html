About

Why do we celebrate St Patrick Day?

The day commemorates Saint Patrick and the arrival of Christianity in Ireland, and celebrates the heritage and culture of the Irish in general. Celebrations generally involve public parades and festivals, céilís, and the wearing of green attire or shamrocks.

What is the story of St Patrick Day?

Saint Patrick's Day, feast day (March 17) of St. Patrick, patron saint of Ireland. Born in Roman Britain in the late 4th century, he was kidnapped at the age of 16 and taken to Ireland as a slave. By the time of his death on March 17, 461, he had established monasteries, churches, and schools.

Why is St Patrick so important?

Saint Patrick is the patron saint of Ireland. He was a Christian missionary given credited with converting Ireland to Christianity in the AD 400s. So many legends surround his life that the truth is not easily found. St Patrick was not actually Irish.

What do you eat on St Patrick's Day?

Celebrate St. Patrick's Day with Irish-inspired foods like corned beef and cabbage, shepherd's pie and Irish soda bread, beef and guinnes pies, classic Irish stew, potato leek soup, Irish apple cake, black and white pudding.

How to play the game?

The game is played with your keyboard. It only accepts the letters A - Z.

All the game questions and answers can be found on the site, or generally online, and as such, we hope you get enjoyment out of the game.

Once you enter a character on your keyboard, the game starts.

If a letter is not within the answer, it'll be added to the letters guessed, and then deduct a guess from the remaining guesses.

Mobile players should click on the underlined section, and the keyboard appears, then thereafter you can start playing the game.

What is the game based on?

The game is based on the original game, called hang-man. You get a certain amount of guesses, and each guess, takes a "life" or "guess" away from your available guesses.

With this said, we hope you get a lot of enjoyment out of the game, and, that you can answer all the options that are possible.
